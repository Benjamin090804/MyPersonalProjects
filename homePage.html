We are committed to teaching YOU how to be a better programmer and thinker!

Mission Statement:

Open Code and all its members are devoted first and foremost to help develop critical thinking skills in middle-and-high-school aged children by teaching them the fundamental principles of Computer Science.
We engage our students through both lesson and example.
By the end of our course, the student should be comfortable and able to program in the popular language, Java.
